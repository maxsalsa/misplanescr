# SYSTEM INSTRUCTIONS: ANTIGRAVITY [OFFICIAL EXAM STRUCTURE]

**1. THE "PRUEBA ESCRITA" ANATOMY (MANDATORY)**
When generating a Written Exam (Prueba Escrita), you must output a structured JSON/Markdown containing:
* **Header:** School Name, Teacher Name, Subject, Period, Total Points, Percentage, Time (min).
* **General Instructions:** Standard MEP rules (Blue/Black ink, no correction fluid, cellphones off).
* **Part I - Selección Única:** Questions with 3 or 4 distractors.
* **Part II - Correspondencia (Pareo):** Two columns logic.
* **Part III - Respuesta Construida:** Short answer or specific resolution.
* **Cognitive Balance:** Ensure items match the cognitive level of the indicator (e.g., If indicator says "Analyze", do not ask a "Cite" question).

**2. RUBRIC DENSITY (THE RULE OF 6)**
* **Requirement:** Every "Tarea Corta", "Proyecto", or "Trabajo Cotidiano" MUST have a rubric with **5 to 6 distinct criteria**.
* **Criteria Examples:**
    1.  Content Mastery (Dominio del tema).
    2.  Punctuality (Puntualidad).
    3.  Follows Instructions (Seguimiento de instrucciones).
    4.  Format/Presentation (Orden y Aseo).
    5.  Argumentation/Depth (Profundidad).
    6.  Use of Sources (Uso de fuentes).
* **Scale:** 1 (Inicial) - 2 (Intermedio) - 3 (Avanzado).

**3. UNIT-BASED PLANNING**
* Plan strictly by Study Unit. The Exam covers the contents of that specific Unit.
